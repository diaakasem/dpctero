Who are we ?

We are digital activists, web developers, video makers, graphic designers and web-analysers from all over the world interested in data mining and analysing data generated by terrorist groups and their sympathisers; we are united to create an original idea consisting in the creation of an online watchdog dealing with a close scrutiny of terrorism advocates on Twitter, Facebook and other social media channels. What are we doing

We are not affiliated to any political party or any ideology and we do not work for the interest of whomever. Our action is non-violent and is based on a volunteering purpose. We intend to visualize public data published on Twitter online with full respect of privacy rules in accordance with law. Graphics, analyses, reports that will be published in the DataShield.com will be by no means for the intention of creating panic or irrational fear but rather an attempt to understand the logic behind the phenomenon of online apology of terror and terrorism propaganda. In this context, if our platform publishes graphics or reports highlighting peaks of terrorist web activities, it does not mean that we predict a terrorist attack. Our role is simply data analysis and data sharing based on the examination of open resources and public postings and we are not claiming or intending to dwell into security matters which are totally out of our reach. Our basic objective is to create a friendly spot where graphics and data could be visible to everyone.

What is DataShield.com in one sentence?

DataShield.com is a data mining podium that analyses open source information published on twitter by people who make apology of terrorism.
